## Overview

We utilize enterprise-grade hardware across all of our servers to deliver consistent, reliable performance. The specific processor assigned to your service depends on current availability and inventory at each location. Hardware configurations may vary between regions.

<Note>
We do not guarantee a specific processor model for any plan, and server migrations solely for the purpose of obtaining a different processor are not supported. Hardware allocations may change over time as newer, more efficient processors become available.
</Note>

## CPU Clock Speeds

All of our servers operate at their respective processors' turbo boost clock speeds. Please keep in mind:

- The CPU clock speed displayed within your VM may be **inaccurate** and could reflect the processor's base clock rather than real-time turbo frequencies.
- Most processors used across our infrastructure boost well beyond **3 GHz+**.
- All servers are properly cooled to maintain sustained performance under heavy load.
